The Mission & Vision Behind It All.

Across the country, colleges provide numerous free valuable resources for students to benefit from – ranging from free subscriptions to prominent platforms to accessible 3D printing centers. Unfortunately, these resources often go unnoticed, with students unaware of their existence. This lack of awareness leads to the wasting of expensive tuition money allocated to support these valuable assets. Our mission is to illuminate these resources, ensuring that students not only become aware of them but also seize the opportunity to utilize them for their diverse needs.
